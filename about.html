About Our Parish

Our Lady of Mercy Miathene Parish was established in 2026 to serve the Catholic community in Meru County. We operate six active outstations and focus on spiritual growth, community service, and self-sustaining projects.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To lead our parishioners into a Christ-centered, inclusive, and self-sustaining community.

Vision: To grow a parish that embodies faith, service, and fellowship.
