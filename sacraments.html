Our Sacraments

Faith, Tradition, and Life at OLM Miathene

Available Sacraments

Baptism

Initiation into the Christian faith for children and adults. Contact the parish office to schedule.

Confirmation

Strengthening of faith through the Holy Spirit. Preparation programs available for youth and adults.

First Holy Communion

Children receive the Body and Blood of Christ. Classes conducted in the parish for preparation.

Marriage

Celebration of the sacrament of matrimony. Couples are guided by parish programs and guidelines.

Confession

Regular reconciliation with God through confession. Available during parish hours and by appointment.

Anointing of the Sick

Spiritual and physical healing for the sick. Contact the parish to request visits or prayers.
